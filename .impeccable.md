# Design Context: Universa Agency Proposals

### Users
- Potential clients (luxury, tech, ecommerce sectors) looking for high-performance digital infrastructure.
- Business owners who value professional, interactive, and transparent development proposals.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Authoritative, Technical, Precise.
- **Tone**: Cinematic, Premium, Secure.
- **Keywords**: High-Performance, Infrastructure, Scalability, Interactive.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Primary Palette**: 
  - Canvas Surface: `#0e131f` (Deep Navy)
  - Accent/Primary: `#2ddc80` (Emerald Green)
  - Text Primary: `#ffffff`
  - Text Secondary: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.6)`
- **Typography**: **VisbyCF** Bold for titles, Med/Regular for body. Modular scale with high contrast.
- **Theme**: Fixed Dark Mode (Luxury/Technical vibe).

### Design Principles
1. **Kinetic Architecture**: Use staggered reveals on all proposal phases to simulate "building" the project.
2. **Glassmorphism 2.0**: Every panel has a 1px emerald border (`rgba(45, 220, 128, 0.1)`) and deep background blur (16px+).
3. **Asymmetric Hierarchy**: Layout headers and metrics with offset margins to avoid the "centered AI template" look.
4. **Physic-Driven UI**: Buttons use spring-physics tactile feedback (`scale(0.98)` on press).
